What is the Strategic Defense Initiative

On a surface level, the SDI is a system designed to protect the United States from nuclear ICBMs in the case of a nuclear first strike by the Soviet Union, or a retaliation strike from the US.
However, in the grand scheme of things, the SDI was said to have 3 final goals.

Protect american Soil

Use a network of satellites or other means to destroy Soviet ICBMs to prevent them from reaching the American Mainland

Rid the world of nukes

Share the system with the Soviet Union, allies, and other nations to turn nukes from an existential threat to an obsolete pieces of junk.
